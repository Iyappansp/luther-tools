Our Founding

From One Workbench
to Ten Thousand

LuthierTools began in a small, dust-filled workshop in the hills of Andalusia. David Arroyo, a third-generation guitar maker, found himself frustrated by the lack of precision tools designed specifically for the delicate nuances of instrument building.

What started as a personal quest to forge the perfect finger plane soon grew into a mission to support the entire luthier community. We didn't just want to sell tools; we wanted to preserve a craft.
