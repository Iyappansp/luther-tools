Our Story

15 years putting the right tools in the hands of master builders

Our Founding

Started in a Workshop,
Built for Workshops

In 2010, master luthier David Arroyo was frustrated. He spent more time sourcing quality tools than actually building. The tools available either weren't designed for instrument work, or cost a fortune for mediocre quality.

So he did what craftsmen do — he solved the problem himself. He partnered with specialist tool makers in Japan, Germany, and the UK to develop tools specifically for luthiery, and shared them with the community.

LuthierTools was born — not as a business first, but as a mission: get the right tools to the right hands so that more beautiful instruments could exist in the world.
