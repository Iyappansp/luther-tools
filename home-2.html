Our Mission

More than Tools.
A Philosophy of Craft.

When David Arroyo started LuthierTools in 2010, the goal was simple: give builders access to tools that actually work — designed with the same obsessive attention to quality that goes into every handmade instrument.

Fifteen years on, that mission hasn't changed. Every tool is field-tested in real workshops on real builds. If it doesn't perform, it doesn't make the cut.
